# Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to foster an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community. The project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its foundation.

## Our Standards

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Respect differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Provide and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Accept responsibility and apologise to those affected by our mistakes.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent repercussions.
